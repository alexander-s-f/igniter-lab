igniter-console — an IDE-shell over a ViewArtifact app

This whole window is the kit's `FrameRuntime` output. It loads lead_review.view.json, runs it, and wraps it: click the frame viewer to drive the app (each interaction records a frame), click a replay chip to time-travel, and watch the lineage + frame diff update.
