Scroll · Tab · Hover

Wheel to scroll the 24-row list (a 10-row window) · move the mouse to highlight a row · Tab/Shift+Tab/↑↓ to move the focus ring (it auto-scrolls into view) · Enter or click to select. Hover and scroll are view-only (they re-render but don't advance the frame index); nav and select are domain effects.
