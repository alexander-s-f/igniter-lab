Deterministic 3D — Ceiling B/C

Five wireframe cubes (one centre + four orbiters), projected through a fixed-point perspective camera and depth-shaded. Lifts the frame-ui audit's Ceiling B (the frame model gains depth + topology + material) and Ceiling C (a fixed-timestep tick drives the loop). Pure integer math, no f64 — a precomputed sin table + integer rotation/perspective — so it's deterministic and bit-identical across machines, and replay is exact.
